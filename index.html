Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection

Converter Pro operates as a pure utility tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information (PII) such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

🔒

All unit conversions are processed entirely locally on your device. No conversion data ever leaves your phone.

2. Third-Party Services

To keep this app free for everyone, Converter Pro uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect and use anonymous data — such as Advertising IDs — to serve relevant ads tailored to your interests.

ℹ️

This data is managed entirely by Google. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for full details on how AdMob handles your information.

3. App Permissions

Converter Pro requests only the permissions strictly necessary for its operation:

🌐

Internet Access — Required only for loading advertisements via AdMob.

🚫

We do not access your contacts, gallery, camera, microphone, or device location.

4. Data Security

Since Converter Pro does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no user data stored on external servers to protect. Your conversions remain private, on-device, at all times.

5. Children's Privacy

Converter Pro is a general-purpose educational tool suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a revised "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at: support@yourdomain.com
